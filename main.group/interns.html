Meet the 2014 Summer Interns

Every summer at Northland, we have interns who come from all over the United States. We're excited we get to host them for eight weeks! The reason we host interns has been the same for over two decades: We believe in investing in the next generation, not just for the contribution they'll make in the future, but for the way they will shape Northland while they're here.

Many Northlanders have been involved in encouraging and shaping the lives of our interns, as well as investing in them financially. We invest approximately $2,000 per summer intern, and it's money well spent! Want to help? You can make a contribution of any size that will help these students with cost of living during their time with us. It's a great opportunity to make an investment in the next generation and the expansion of God's Kingdom. That's because starting this summer, these students will receive training on how to plant churches. Our goal is to continue working with them after they go back to their campuses and see what God might doâ€”perhaps start a church in their dorm or where they live. So it's an opportunity to invest in church planting as well.

You can help sponsor this program by making a tax-deductible donation to Northland's True North Foundation, which provides grants in the areas of the arts, education and entrepreneurial ministry.
