Ava Belle Hunter, sweet and much loved daughter, wonderful sister, loving granddaughter, niece, cousin and friend, went to be with Jesus on September 4, 2010.

Ava was born on November 1, 2004, in Orlando. She is survived by her loving parents, Josh and Lisa Hunter, her hero of a big brother, Noah, proud grandparents, Joel and Becky Hunter, grandmother Patty Gable and grandfathers Guy Doman and Bill Gable; uncles, Isaac Hunter, Joel Hunter and Paul Doman and aunts, Rhonda Hunter and Elizabeth Hunter; and cousins Jada, Ella, Lincoln and Luke Hunter, and Tyler Doman.

Ava loved learning about Jesus and spending time with her family.

In lieu of flowers, the family is asking for memorials to be given online http://bit.ly/avafund or to the Ava Hunter Fund, Citizens Bank of Florida, Attn: Jolene Burns. P.O. Box 620729, Oviedo, FL 32762-0729.
