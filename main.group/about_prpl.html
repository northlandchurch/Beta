Northland Is

a Church of churches, a family of spiritual families.

We believe Church is more than a building—it’s a network of spiritual families. Our relationships form the church, not the walls. Three-in-one, God Himself is a relationship; He is Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Building a church-of-churches allows us to more fully reflect His image and walk alongside one another on the path to spiritual maturity. 

As members of one spiritual family, we take the church to people where they are — everywhere, everyday. We don’t just go to church; we DO church. Through boots-on-the-ground ministries, Northland goes "out there" to include the not yet included. We believe by connecting with those who are different than us, we accomplish more for His kingdom. 

A church starts with one person with a heart and yearning for Jesus.
Start where YOU are today — get connected.

The Distributed Church

As a distributed church, Northland connects with individuals and organizations outside its four walls, worshiping and serving together everywhere, every day. While most Northlanders gather at our Central Florida sites, many also gather in house churches, in prisons and online. Our global partnerships provide mutual opportunities to connect outside our own cultures and to partner together in building the Kingdom of God.
