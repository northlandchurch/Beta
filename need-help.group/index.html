Need help?

LifeHope

Northland's benevolence ministry, LifeHope, provides assistance through a holistic approach. LifeHope Advocates work together with individuals and families to set personal goals and strengthen connections to provide support, including the local church.

Request LifeHope support

Counseling

Each of us faces overwhelming situations or events on the path to spiritual maturity, and we are here to help navigate life's obstacles. Northland offers Christian counseling services to help guide congregants through the struggles of daily and spiritual life. Our counselors combine clinical and Christian principles, offering encouragement, comfort, and support to facilitate spiritual and emotional healing.

Need help finding counseling that's right for you? Call Northland's counseling referral line at 407-949-7204

Request counseling support

Hospital Visits

Should a member of our spiritual family need to stay in the hospital or a rehab facility, we want to walk alongside them through what can be a difficult time. Our Hospital Support Team will visit you or your loved one, offering prayer, words of encouragement and healing, and emotional support. During times of sickness or injury, let this team serve as your point of connection and support.

Request a hospital visit

Pastoral Care

Northland has a NEW Pastoral Care Team, which consists of pastors, elders, ministers and church leaders. We are here to shepherd the flock at Northland together (John 21:15-17). We care deeply about your needs and your questions, and we are available to meet with you one-on-one or in group settings to listen, to pray, to offer counsel and to help make connections to resources within Northland's family. If you would like to spend some time with someone from the Pastoral Care Team, please do not hesitate to ask. It is why we are here!
