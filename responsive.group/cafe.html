Nature's Table Cafe

Cafe Hours

  • Monday through Thursday, 11 a.m. - 7 p.m.
  • Closed Friday, except during special events
  • Saturday, 4 - 8 p.m.
  • Sunday , 8 a.m. - 2 p.m.; 5 - 7 p.m.

Located in Northland’s lobby, Nature’s Table Cafe is open to the public throughout the week! It’s the perfect place to connect with friends and family before or after services, to meet with your Bible study or other group, or to enjoy a great meal anytime.

Fresh sandwiches, salads and soups as well as smoothies are served daily. The restaurant can even be reserved for private events. Talk to Chef Mike about party platters or catering your next event (407-937-1781). FREE WI-FI!
